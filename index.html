All In One Health Understanding

The Corevity App makes health simple and actionable. It automates and aggregates your health. It's designed to help you understand your health and get on with your life. Never manually input your health events again. We will add your nutrition, exercise and sleep data from your favorite apps to aggregate your health experience.

We will update you throughout the day of your progress and make personalized recommendations to improve your health. Download the free app and change how you feel today.

How It Works

1
Automated

Logging your nutrition, sleep & exercise data is a waste of time. Corevity does it for you. Connect your relevant apps once and never do it again! It’s easy, straightforward and secure.

2
Actionable

Understand your health performance in seconds. Stop counting calories or steps. We take care of that for you and show an understandable health score that encompasses the most revenant health inputs: sleep, nutrition & exercise.

3
Personalized

Personalized actionable health coaching and goal reminders. We will send you friendly reminders throughout the day so you know how you’re tracking towards your health goals and give you advice when you need it most.
