Technology

Understanding your health can be frustrating. Scientific studies are conflicting. One day eggs are good for you, the next day they aren't. Who can keep up? The base nutrition philosophy of the early 90's, The Food Pyramid, has been shattered and replaced with the "Farm to Table" movement. So what's next? Data tracking of health related events have exploded. Today, 1 in 5 Americans own a wearable and by 2019, 149 million units will be sold each year. Sadly, wearable data lacks insight and personalized recommendations to make you healthier. You are left with a pretty watch or band, but little else.

The opportunity to understand health data is here, let's give users control of their health. The next step is to use that data to provide useful health insights, to improve health understanding and decrease health costs. Our technology makes all of this possible. Don't wait for the future of health, Corevity is bringing it to you.

To learn how we got here, check out our About Page. To see where we are going, glimpse into our technology below.

Corevity created the first real-time health scoring model. We aggregate, automate, categorize and score your health experience creating easy to understand health data.

  • Integration with your existing health, wearable and fitness apps. We collect data from the apps and bands you are already using and wearing. This includes exercise and sleep data. The first time you sign in, we can provide immediately actionable data.

  • Nutrition Data that doesn't require food logging! We have taken a completely new approach to tracking nutrition data. We collect nutrition data from your food purchases by linking your debit and credit card accounts. Never waste your time logging your food ever again.

  • Categorization of your key health inputs. After collecting your sleep, exercise and nutrition data, we help further qualify it's value by categorizing it individually and as an aggregate. This not only deepens the meaning of the original data, but allows for cross input indexing, unlocking new health insights from previously unactionable data.

  • Scoring your health. We provide an easy to understand score for both category and aggregate health data that can we sorted by time. You can track your process throughout the day and compare your health performance to better understand the results.

  • Personalization of your health. We take these inputs a step further and offer personalized recommendations to initiate health understanding at the individual level.

Our data model is beyond anything the industry has seen. By removing manual input(s) we have limited any biased data sets, thus allowing for true real-time health profiles. The uniformity of our data content creates opportunity to remove the walls that exist in current health research and understanding.
