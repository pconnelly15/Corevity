Technology

Understanding your health can be a daunting task. The movement of data and data belief is moving faster than consumers can keep up with. Compound this issue with scientific revision upon revision on top of each other. The base nutrition philosophy of the early 90's, The Food Pyramid, has already been shattered and replaced with a "Farm to Table" movement. So what's next? At the same time, data tracking of health related events have exploded. Before 2019, 1 in 10 Americans will own a wearable (149 Million Units!). The ability to itemize events in our lives is only at its infancy.

Even with all of this data, the lack of true correlation and relationship of individual health inputs has remained elusive. The importance of these relationships create not only scientific but deeply personal significance. They impact how you feel, how you are with yourself and how other see you. Health isn't an intellectual exercise, its at the base of mere existence.
