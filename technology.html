Technology

Corevity is the first fully automated health tracker. Corevity tracks the data that influences your health profile: nutrition, exercise, and sleep. We aggregate, categorize, and score your health experience. We aggregate your existing health data and categorize your nutrition data from credit/debit card purchases. The result is an easy to understand score from 1-100.

To learn how we got here, check out our About Page, to glimpse into our technology see below.

How it works

  • Integration with your existing health, wearable and fitness apps. We collect data from the apps and bands you are already wearing. This includes exercise and sleep data. From the first time your sign in, we can provide immediately actionable data.

  • Nutrition Data that doesn't require food logging! We collect nutrition data from your food purchases by linking your debit/credit card account. Never waste your time logging your food ever again.

  • Categorization of your key health inputs. After collecting your health data, we help further qualify it's value by categorizing it individually and as an aggregate. This deepens the meaning of the original data and allows for cross input indexing, unlocking new health insights from previously unactionable data

  • Scoring of your health with an easy to understand score from 1-100. We provide an easy to understand score for both category and aggregate health data that can we sorted by time. Users can track their process throughout the day and compare their health performance to better understand their results.

  • Personalization of your health. We take these inputs a step further and offer personalized recommendations to help you reach your health goals.

Our data is a better version of a FICO score, but for health. It's the only real-time health view for individuals. This data will redefine healthcare. Corevity is the place to share your health journey.
