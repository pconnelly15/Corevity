Our Story

Health is top of mind. Cost is rising (per capita). Empowerment is unachievable. Too Many questions, not enough answers. To fix this, Corevity has developed the first-ever aggregated consumer health model through our Proprietary Health Input Graph giving you easy and automated health scoring and understanding at your fingertips. On trail, in gym or at home see your real time health data in seconds so you enhance your health and improve your life.

Corevity is the first automated health technology platform designed to understand and correlate the most important health factors so you feel your best. We look at the 3 keys health inputs that influence your health profile: what you eat, how you exercise and when you sleep. We automatically track this data for you, because who has time and who can remember, and remind you what you might be missing so you feel like the best YOU. Its not just data science, its health understanding connected to your life.

Health understanding is the key to living a longer, better life. But the right ways to do this have been too confusing to easily misunderstand. Corevity takes care of that for you, so you can understand your health and get on with your life.
