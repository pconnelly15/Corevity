Our story

How can we simplify health understanding? We need to move health from obscure to simple. Today, health empowerment is unachievable. There are too many questions, and not enough answers. On top of that, health data isn't standardized and healthcare costs are crushing our financial future. Here is the question we need to answer:

What is the value of waking up rested, feeling good every morning. How can we quantify this, then replicate it?

To solve this, Corevity has developed the first-ever fully automated consumer health model. It makes health simple, automated and personalized.

Corevity is the first automated health technology platform designed to understand the most important health factors in your life. Our health model was developed over a 10 year period to focus on the most influential health factors. We look at the 3 keys health inputs that influence your health profile: nutrition, exercise and sleep. We automatically track this data for you, by aggregating your existing health data. We provide easy to understand health scores and personalized tips to make you healthier.

Health needs to be simple, beautiful and easy to understand. It needs to be able to go with you in any situation. This is Corevity.

I thought I was healthy. I am a high intensity, mesomorphic person. I played college sports. I have always been an active guy. When I was 22 I was training 6 days a week, 3+ hours a day. I was extremely fit. I was an example athlete.

I found out I wasn’t healthy. When I was 26, I developed high blood pressure (140/100) that exposed my lack of health understanding (my favorite food was sandwiches). I thought I was the American vision of health, yet my health score was a D-. Rather than incur the cost and terror of taking pills, I changed my lifestyle. I didn’t want to change my activity levels so I started manually testing my nutrition inputs to increase my energy levels. I moved from processed food to cleaner, whole foods (meat & veggies) and now mostly organic (high vegetable, and low meat diet.). There was still no barometer or feedback system. It was all trial and error.

I wanted to solve this problem. It was all experimentation for me. There was no direct path. I had to test and test and test to get here. Scientific reports were conflicting, and diets fabs were trending and falling faster then I could keep up with. It took me 10 years to fully hone my health philosophy. I created Corevity to make health simple, beautiful and actionable for everyone.

-Corevity Founder, Patrick Connelly
