3RD PARTY APP INTERGRATION

What other fitness or sleep apps integrate into Corevity?

Any wearables or apps that integrates with Apple Health is available for Corevity to better understand your activity and sleep data. In addition to adding your Apple Health data, we have integrated with FitBit as well. Use your FitBit (any version) and we will automatically sync your step and sleep data into Corevity.

What data does Corevity collect?

Activity and sleep data that flows through Apple Health or Fitbit is sourced into our health graph. We use that data to calculate your health score, we don’t store your health data, just your health score. Corevity uses your Credit or Debit card info to understand your food purchase history and calculate your nutrition data. We use that data to calculate your health score, we don’t store your health data, just your health score.
